{# ============================================================ #} {# STORY #} {# ============================================================ #}

Our story

Built by guides, run for travellers.

We started in 2014 with one Land Cruiser, three guides, and a stubborn idea: that the best Tanzania trips aren't the ones with the longest brochure — they're the ones built around the people on them.

Ten years later we're a small Arusha-based team running bespoke safaris, Kilimanjaro climbs, and Zanzibar extensions for travellers who want the trip to feel like a story they choose to tell, not a checklist they tick off.

We don't run group tours. We don't share itineraries between clients. Every trip is designed from the ground up around your dates, pace, and the parks you actually want to see.

{# ============================================================ #} {# VALUES #} {# ============================================================ #}

What we stand for

Three things we won't budge on.

Local first

Every itinerary is built and led by guides who grew up walking these parks. No outsourcing to remote agents.

Bespoke always

No fixed groups, no shared itineraries. Each trip is yours — your dates, your pace, your wishlist.

Honest pricing

What we quote is what you pay. Park fees, conservation levies, and tips for the team are spelled out up front.
