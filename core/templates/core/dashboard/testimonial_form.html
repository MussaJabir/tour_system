Testimonial Best Practices
  • Be authentic: Use real customer feedback whenever possible.
  • Include specifics: Mention specific destinations, activities, or experiences.
  • Keep it concise: 2-3 sentences is ideal for web display.
  • Add photos: Customer photos add credibility and personality.
  • Highlight results: Focus on outcomes and experiences, not just descriptions.
  • Feature the best: Mark your strongest testimonials as featured for homepage display.
